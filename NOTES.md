Required confidence grading:

- A: survey / LiDAR / stamped CAD-BIM / controlled metric source.
- B: controlled photogrammetry with GCPs or known scale.
- C: photo-calibrated schematic geometry or manual dimensions with known references.
- D: visual-only / conceptual / unverified geometry.

Photo and concept-rendering rule:

Photos and concept renderings are not quantity evidence unless calibrated with known dimensions, survey, LiDAR, or controlled photogrammetry.
